About Us

At Hawas, we believe great coffee begins at the source.
That’s why we work directly with some of the finest farms in Vietnam, where generations of growers dedicate themselves to cultivating beans of exceptional quality. Every batch of coffee we offer is carefully roasted in small quantities, ensuring ultimate freshness, rich aroma, and a taste that truly stands out. Our goal is simple: to bring you coffee that not only delights your senses but also gives you the energy and focus to carry through your day. As a small, passionate company, we take pride in every step of the journey—from farm to roast to your cup. With us, coffee isn’t just a product. It’s a craft, a connection, and a daily ritual worth savoring.
